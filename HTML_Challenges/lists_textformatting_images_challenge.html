Chicken-Schnitzel


Schnitzel means cutlet in German, and is a popular deep-fried and breaded dish enjoyed in many countries throughout the world. By following this easy recipe you can make scrumptious chicken schnitzel for dinner – always a winner with kids in particular

Prep time: 15 mins

Cook time: 20 mins

Total time: 35 mins

Serves: 2-4 Servings


<

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken fillets
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 150g breadcrumbs
  • butter or cooking oil

Directions

  1. Tenderise the chicken with a meat mallet.
  2. Season the flour with salt and pepper.
  3. Coat the chicken pieces in the flour.
  4. Beat the eggs.
  5. Coat the chicken in the egg mixture and place immediately into the breadcrumbs.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and fry the schnitzel on both sides until brown and the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Alternatively the schnitzel can be baked in an oven for 15-20 minutes at 180°C.

To Summarise

Tenderise the chicken, then coat it in flour, then the egg mixture. Cover the chicken in breadcrumbs immediately after the egg mixture. Now fry the chicken in the pan with oil until it is brown on both sides and the chicken is cooked through, or bake in the oven for 15 - 20 minutes at 180 degrees.
